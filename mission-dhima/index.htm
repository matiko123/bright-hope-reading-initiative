OUR MISSION

To have a society where by young learners are encouraged and empowered to educational reading culture.

DHIMA YETU

Kuwa na jamii ambayo vijana wapenda kusoma hupewa motisha na nguvu juu ya elimu ya tamadduni ya usomaji.

About Us

BRIGHT HOPE READING INITIATIVE is aware of problem facing young learner’s community that including, poor access to educational reading clubs or libraries, lack of financial support in funding new books, libraries and clubs, lack of awareness in importance of reading as educational learning fundamental as the results of poor literacy ability, communication skills, reading and writing among young learners.
